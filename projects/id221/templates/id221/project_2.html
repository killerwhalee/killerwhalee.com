📷 Still, we love film cameras

With the spread of digital cameras and smartphones, no one will use film cameras.

A film camera that is heavy, expensive, and can't be checked right away looks really uncomfortable.

But there are people who still use film cameras.

Why do they love film so much?

Ideation

Idea 1: How to Cook a Photo → Why Analog Photography?

Introducing full process of analog photography

  1. Goal - Introduce people how picture was made in the past
  2. Audience - People used to take photo by just clicking shutter button on their phone
  3. Message - Try enjoying slow process to “cook” photography
  4. Story - In these days people are spamming photos by using phone since no efforts are needed anymore for get image of what we see. Since I enjoy taking film photography, I want people to enjoy film photo and experience value of its slow but meticulous process to get a photo.
  5. Data - There's no statistics in this theme, but still its contents has many data inside it. I have to represent it efficiently.
  6. Contents -
    • Taking photo: About shutter, aperture; Format of film; Type of film
    • Developing: Process to develop; time and temperature;
    • Printing: Contrast and exposure; Process to print;

Idea Sketch

Idea 2: Know Your Pasta

Introducing various different pasta

  1. Goal - To introduce people various type of pasta and make people differentiate them.
  2. Audience - Someone who always say “spaghetti” to every pasta.
  3. Message - Know what you actually eat and explore various pasta.
  4. Story - I really love cooking pasta as well as eating pasta. I can cook tens of different pasta, but I always get mad when people say “what a delicious looking spaghetti”. Spaghetti is name of noodle, not a name of food.Though western cuisine is prevalent in current korean society, but still pasta is not well-specified and noticed. There are tens of different types of noodle and sauce in pasta recipe and it would be much fun to “know exactly what you eat” !
  5. Data - There are many types of noodle and sauce for pasta. I'm going to classify each type of noodle and sauce separately. For noodle, I'm planning to sort by its shape, ingredients or recommended sauce type. For sauce, I'll make a tree graph to make people find their favorite sauce.We can also put statistics about arising amount of pasta restaurant or net revenue of instant pasta products.
  6. Contents
    • Noodle: Type of noodle; Selecting good noodle; Good pairing with sauce; Cook noodle
    • Sauce: Type of sauce; Finding my favorite sauce for dinner
    • Cooking: How to cook pasta delicious;

그럼에도 우리가 필름을 사랑하는 이유?
  1. 신중함
    • (하지만) 단 36장, 사진 확인 못 함, 필름은 가격도 비싸..
        [통계1: 사진 통계] [통계2: 평균 필름 가격] [통계3: gb당 sd카드 가격]
    • (그럼에도) 사진 한 장을 찍을 때도 고민하고 생각하게 만듦
  2. 정성
    • (하지만) 필름에 상을 담아내고, 그것을 다시 종이로 옮겨내야 함.며칠이 걸리는 작업인 데다가 비용도 발생함
    • (그럼에도) 사진을 ‘찍는다'는 느낌보다 ‘만든다'의 느낌에 가까움사진 한 장을 얻기 위해 더 많은 노력과 정성이 들어가므로, 그만큼 더 소중하다.
  3. 색감
    • (하지만) 필름은 비싸다. SD카드는 요새 만원밖에 안 하던데…
    • (그럼에도) 보정이 필요 없는 필름만의 따뜻한 분위기감도나 필름의 종류에 따른 분위기를 즐기는 재미
  4. 개성
    • (하지만) 무겁고 조작할 것도 많다.
      • [통계1: 평균 스마트폰 무게][통계2: minolta 카메라 차트]
      • 평균 스마트폰 무게: 150~180g
      • 평균 SLR 카메라 무게: 500~900g (렌즈 없이)
    • (그럼에도) 카메라 별로 온갖 다양한 디자인이 있어 개성이 넘친다.
데이터 스케치
  1. 필름은 비싸다: 한 번밖에 못 쓰고, 적은 사진에 가격이 비싸다
  2. 필름은 느리다: 찍자마자 확인도 못 하고, 며칠을 기다려야 한다.
  3. 필름은 무겁다: 카메라가 금속으로 만들어져 있어 무겁다.

그럼에도 필름을 하는 이유는

  1. 필름은 부드럽다: 노이즈 대신 풍부한 그레인이 있고, 색감이 따뜻하다
  2. 필름은 개성있다: 다양한 종류의 카메라가 있고 디자인이 개성있다.
  3. 필름은 소중하다: 신중하고 오랜 시간 정성들여 만든 만큼 사진에 소중함과 애정이 생긴다.
